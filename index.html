I'm an assistant professor in Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT). I received my doctoral and bachelor degrees from Peking University in 2020 and 2015, co-advised by Prof. Gang Huang and Prof. Xuanzhe Liu. I was a visiting scholar at Purdue ECE in 2019, working with Prof. Felix Xiaozhu Lin . I interned at MSRA System group from Mar. 2015 to Mar. 2016, mentored by Dr. Yunxin Liu.

News: I will visit MSRA again from Dec. 2020 to join the "star-track" program.

I always look for highly self-motivated undergraduates and graduates. Please directly send your CV to me if you find my research interesting.

Research

I work on systems software, with focus on resource-constrained platforms like mobile and edge:
  • AI systems on resource-constrained devices, e.g., smartphones, wearables, and IoT cameras. We are maintaining a paper list for this area. Check it out!
    [MobiSys'20] autonomous camera, [WWW'19] empirical study, [TMC'19] wearable-smartphone orchestration, [MobiCom'18] vision cache, [UbiComp'18] on-device learning, [arxiv] zero-streaming camera.
  • Designing and optimizing mobile systems for better performance, privacy, and user experience.
    [UbiComp'18] input privacy, [EuroSys'18] power awareness, [WWW'17] app collusion, [TMC'17] mobile resource scheduling.
  • Designing new federated learning platforms.
    [WWW'21] Heterogeneity-aware, [arxiv] LAN-aware, [arxiv] self-adaptive.
