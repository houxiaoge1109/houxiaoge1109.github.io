I'm an assistant professor in Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT). I received my doctoral and bachelor degrees from Peking University in 2020 and 2015, co-advised by Prof. Gang Huang and Prof. Xuanzhe Liu. I was a visiting scholar at Purdue ECE in 2019, working with Prof. Felix Xiaozhu Lin . I interned at MSRA System group from Mar. 2015 to Mar. 2016, mentored by Dr. Yunxin Liu.

I always look for highly self-motivated undergraduates and graduates. Please directly send your CV to me if you find my research interesting.

Research

I work on computer systems and softwares, with focus on resource-constrained platforms like mobile, edge, and IoT devices. I am recently building edge AI systems that achieve good performance and privacy.
